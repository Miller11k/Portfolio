About Me

Since I was a child I have been researching how electronics work and how they were able to connect those around the world. Since then I have dedicated my life to developing inventions that I feel would have an impact on those around me (see "Past Projects" for more detailed information). To me, engineering is the bridge that connects magic that we could only dream of to the everyday world. I graduated from Newton South High School with a certification from the engineering department and am currently an undergraduate student at Purdue University's Elmore Family School of Electrical and Computer Engineering where I hope to graduate with a major in computer engineering and a minor in economics.
